Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe to buy gold and silver bullion online in Australia?

Yes. Reputable Australian bullion dealers operate with transparent pricing, clear product descriptions, and secure delivery methods. Buyers should always confirm pricing currency, metal purity, and shipping terms before purchasing.

How is gold and silver bullion priced in AUD?

Bullion pricing is typically based on the live spot price of the metal, converted into Australian dollars, with a premium applied to cover minting, handling, and dealer costs. Prices can change as spot markets move.

Do bullion prices change daily?

Yes. Gold and silver prices fluctuate continuously based on global markets, currency movements, and supply and demand. Online bullion prices are updated regularly to reflect these changes.

What purity should I look for when buying bullion?

Most investment-grade gold bullion is 99.5% to 99.99% pure, while silver bullion is commonly 99.9% or higher. Purity is always stated clearly on legitimate bullion products.

What is the difference between bullion and collectible coins?

Bullion is purchased primarily for its metal content and investment value, while collectible or numismatic coins may carry additional value based on rarity, condition, or demand. Bullion pricing tracks metal value more closely.
